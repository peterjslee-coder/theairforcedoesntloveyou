The Weekend Lineup

Three days of celebration, memories, and one last flight

Thursday • May 28
✈️

Simulators & The Taj Tour

Daytime

Get behind the stick one more time (virtually) and take a tour of the Taj. See what Juice has been up to all these years. Fair warning: 134 steps to the top, and it will be approximately one billion degrees until you reach the top where there's a nice breeze and a great view.

🍴

Welcome Dinner at Lee House

Evening

If you're here, the Lee family would love to have you over to our home to get that full Randolph AFB experience. Great food, great company, and plenty of stories.

Friday • May 29 — The Main Event
🛫

The Final Flight

1000 HRS

Colonel Lee takes to the skies one last time in an Air Force aircraft. A career of service, signed off in style.

🍺

Lunch in the 435th Fighter Training Squadron

After Final Flight

Debrief over lunch in the squadron. Cold drinks, hot takes, and a few tales that may or may not be exaggerated.

🎖

Retirement Ceremony

1400 HRS • Parr Club “Auger Inn”

A brief ceremony to mark the end of one era and the beginning of another for the entire Lee family.

🎰 Will Juice cry during his own retirement ceremony?

🎉

Party in the Auger Inn Bar

After Ceremony

The party continues! Raise a glass (or several) to the Lee family's incredible career adventures together.

Saturday • May 30
⛳️

Tubing, Golf & Gruene

All Day • New Braunfels

Wind down with a Texas-sized day of fun. Float the river, hit the links, or explore historic Gruene. Pick your adventure!
